Courses Offered

National Islamic School & Jamia PU College

Pre-Primary (LKG & UKG)

+

Our Pre-Primary program focuses on building strong foundational skills through joyful learning experiences.

  • Play-based learning methodology
  • Storytelling & rhymes
  • Motor skill and sensory development
  • Introduction to letters, numbers & colors
  • Social and emotional growth

Primary School (1st to 5th Standard)

+

This stage focuses on academic basics and character development.

  • Reading, writing & numeracy skills
  • Environmental studies & general knowledge
  • Moral values and discipline
  • Creative arts & physical education
  • Continuous assessment system

Middle School (6th to 8th Standard)

+

Students receive deeper academic exposure with practical learning.

  • Science with lab activities
  • Mathematics & analytical thinking
  • Basic computer education
  • Project-based learning
  • Personality & confidence development

High School (9th & 10th Standard)

+

Board-oriented education with strong academic focus.

  • Experienced faculty & exam guidance
  • Regular tests & revision sessions
  • Career counseling support
