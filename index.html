About National Islamic School

At National Islamic School, we believe education goes beyond the classroom. Founded in 1995, we are committed to nurturing young minds through a holistic approach that blends academic excellence, moral values, and co-curricular enrichment.

Our dedicated faculty and staff work hand-in-hand to provide a safe, inclusive, and inspiring environment for students. From smart classrooms and well-equipped labs to vibrant cultural programs and sports activities, we prepare our students not just for exams, but for life.

We strive to instill confidence, curiosity, and compassion in every learner, empowering them to become responsible citizens and lifelong learners. With a legacy of over 25 years, we continue to inspire and shape the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Academic Programs

From nurturing toddlers to preparing teens, we support every stage of learning.

Pre-Primary (LKG & UKG)

Foundational learning through play-based activities, storytelling, and motor skill development.

Primary (1st to 5th)

Core subjects with emphasis on reading, writing, numeracy and moral values.

Middle School (6th to 8th)

Deeper academic exposure with science labs, basic computer training, and projects.

High School (9th & 10th)

Board exam-focused learning with advanced curriculum, mentoring and career guidance.

Message from the Principal

Dear Parents and Students, I am delighted to welcome you to National Islamic School. Here, we believe that education is not just about academics, but about nurturing curiosity, confidence, and creativity in every child.

Our teachers are committed to inspiring young minds to explore, question, and discover. Every student is encouraged to reach their full potential in a safe and supportive environment.

At National Islamic School, we aim to instill strong values, responsibility, and leadership qualities. We celebrate each child’s uniqueness and guide them to grow into compassionate, capable, and confident individuals.
